On first three levels:
	They are good if:
		They lose 20hp or less (1 projectile, or 2 enemies)
		AND They beat all 3 levels with 155 or more amount of time left total
	They are bad if:
		They lose 40hp or more (4 enemies, 2 projectiles and 1 enemy, etc.)
		OR They beat all 3 levels with 125 or less amount of time left total
		OR They die at all in those levels
	Else they are a regular player
Rest of game:
	If they are good:
		Multiply difficulty of levels by 1.2
	If they are bad:
		Divide difficulty of levels by 1.2
	Else:
		Keep difficulty the same
